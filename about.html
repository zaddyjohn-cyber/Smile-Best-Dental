Our Story

Built on a Belief in Better Smiles

Haysa Dental Clinic was founded in 2012 with a simple but powerful mission: every Kano resident deserves access to world-class dental care — without fear, without hidden fees, and without compromise.

Over a decade later, we have grown into one of Kano's most respected dental practices. Our team of experienced, specialist dentists use the latest digital X-ray technology, LED whitening systems, and precision implant tools to deliver outstanding results. Whether it's your first checkup or a complete smile makeover, we treat every patient like family.

Conveniently located in Fagge, Kano, we are open 24 Hours, 7 Days a Week. Walk-ins are always welcome, and same-day appointments are often available.
