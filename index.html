About Us

Lagos's Most Trusted Dental Clinic

Founded in 2012, Smile Best Dental Clinic has grown into one of Ikeja's most respected dental practices — built on a simple belief: every patient deserves a healthy, beautiful smile without fear or financial stress.

Our team of qualified, experienced dentists use the latest digital X-ray technology, LED whitening systems and precision implant tools to deliver world-class results right here in Ogba, Lagos. Whether it's your first checkup or a full smile makeover, we treat every patient like family.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Our professional LED whitening is completely safe and virtually painless. Some patients experience mild sensitivity for 24 hours after treatment, which passes quickly. We use a protective gel on your gums to keep you comfortable throughout.
With proper care, dental implants can last a lifetime. The titanium post fuses permanently with your jawbone, making it the most durable tooth replacement option available. Regular checkups and good oral hygiene are all you need.
Our pricing is transparent and competitive for Lagos. Costs vary by treatment — teeth whitening starts from ₦35,000, veneers from ₦60,000 per tooth, and implants from ₦250,000. We offer flexible payment plans so cost is never a barrier to your smile.
Treatment time depends on the complexity of your case. Most patients complete their orthodontic journey in 12–24 months. Clear aligners can sometimes be faster for mild to moderate cases. We'll give you a precise timeline at your free consultation.
